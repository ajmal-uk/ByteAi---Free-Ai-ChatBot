Is Byte AI free to use? +

Yes, Byte AI offers a completely free tier with full access to all core features. We also offer premium plans with additional capabilities for power users and businesses. Our goal is to make advanced AI assistance accessible to everyone.

Do I need to create an account to use Byte AI? +

No, Byte AI does not require account creation or signup. You can start using our AI assistant immediately without providing any personal information. This is part of our commitment to privacy and accessibility.

How does Byte AI protect my privacy? +

Byte AI is built on a privacy-first architecture. We don't collect personal data, don't require accounts, and don't store your conversations on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage technologies like localStorage and IndexedDB.

Byte AI does not store your conversations on our servers. All conversation data is stored locally on your device using browser storage technologies. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through your browser settings.
