Privacy Policy

At Byte AI, we believe your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how we protect your data with our privacy-first approach - no data collection, no server storage, and no account system.

Try Byte AI Now

Our Privacy Commitment

Byte AI is built on a foundation of privacy and user control. Unlike many AI services, we don't collect your personal data, don't require accounts, and don't store your conversations on our servers. Your data stays on your device, where it belongs.

Key Privacy Principles: No data collection • No server storage • No account system • Full user control

Data Collection

Byte AI does not collect any personal data from users. We have implemented a privacy-first architecture that eliminates the need for data collection:

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Tracking: We don't track how you use Byte AI or store your interaction history.
  • No Device Fingerprinting: We don't create unique identifiers for your device or browser.
  • No Analytics: We don't use analytics tools that collect data about your usage patterns.

Data Storage

All data associated with your use of Byte AI is stored locally on your device:

  • Local Storage: Your conversations and preferences are stored in your browser's local storage.
  • IndexedDB: For larger data needs, we use IndexedDB, which is entirely on your device.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers.
  • User Control: You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

Byte AI uses only one cookie for a limited purpose:

  • byte_visited: This cookie tracks whether you've seen the welcome screen. It doesn't collect any personal information and is stored locally on your device.
  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes.
  • Optional Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of the welcome screen.

User Rights

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • Access: You can access all your data through your browser's developer tools.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and IndexedDB.
  • Export: You can export your conversation history using browser developer tools.
  • Correction: You can modify your data directly through the application interface.

Third-Party Services

Byte AI integrates with third-party AI models to provide its functionality:

  • Gemini AI Model: We use Google's Gemini AI model for processing your requests. Your prompts are sent to this model for processing, but are not stored by Google or us.
  • No Data Retention: We have configured our integration with third-party services to ensure no data retention.
  • Privacy Policies: While we ensure our integrations are privacy-focused, we recommend reviewing the privacy policies of third-party services for complete information.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Local Data Protection: Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security measures.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Children's Privacy

Byte AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
