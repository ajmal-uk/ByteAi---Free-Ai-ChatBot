Our Mission

We believe in privacy, accessibility, and continuous innovation. Byte AI is built on the principles of user privacy (no data storage), free access for all, and constant improvement to stay at the forefront of AI technology.

Technology Stack

The technologies powering Byte AI

Gemini AI Model

Byte AI is powered by Google's Gemini AI model, providing state-of-the-art natural language processing and reasoning capabilities.

Multi-modal Architecture

Our system uses a multi-modal architecture for processing and context management, enabling seamless integration of text, images, and web data.

Privacy-First Design

All data is stored locally in the user's browser using local storage and IndexedDB, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
